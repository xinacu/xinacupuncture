# xinacupuncture
Xin Wang
L.Ac,  MD (China)
Dr. Xin Wang is a licensed acupuncturist and medical doctor (China) specializing in relieving pain, emotional health, stress, internal medicine, and sports rehabilitation. With extensive experience as a team physician for the Chinese National Gymnastics Team, he integrates Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) to help patients restore balance, improve performance, and live without pain. His treatments combine acupuncture, electroacupuncture, cupping, tuina (medical massage), and individualized herbal recommendations. Dr. Wang’s approach emphasizes both symptom relief and long-term wellness, tailored to each patient’s body condition and lifestyle.
